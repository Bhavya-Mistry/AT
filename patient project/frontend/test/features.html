Everything you need,

ClinIQ is purpose-built for healthcare — every feature exists to improve your care, protect your data, or save you time.

Core experience

AI-Powered Consultation

The centrepiece of ClinIQ — an intelligent intake and triage system that works around the clock.

Multi-language AI intake

Describe symptoms in English or Hinglish. Our AI understands conversational language and guides clinical intake with precision.

Priority-based triage

AI assigns 1-10 priority scores based on symptoms. Urgent cases are flagged immediately for doctor review.

Doctor appointment booking

Schedule consultations with verified doctors. Calendar integration ensures convenient time slots for both parties.

Structured medical summaries

AI generates clinical summaries for doctors. Includes symptoms, history, vitals, and priority assessment.

Records & files

Your Digital Health Record

A permanent, portable, patient-controlled health record that grows with every interaction.

Unified timeline

Every consultation, prescription, and uploaded file in one chronological view.

Google Drive integration

Files uploaded to secure Google Drive storage. Audio files are automatically transcribed for medical context.

Compliance tracking

BAA signing and privacy policy acceptance tracked in user profiles. Ensures regulatory compliance.

Health profile

Store your medical history, allergies, current medications, and chronic conditions for instant context in every consultation.

Security & privacy

Built for Healthcare Privacy

HIPAA-aligned security

Built with healthcare privacy standards including audit logging and BAA support for covered entities.

Bcrypt password security

Industry-leading password hashing with JWT authentication. Google OAuth option available for seamless access.

HIPAA audit logging

Every access to patient data is logged. Tracks who accessed what, when, and for what purpose.

Role-based access control

PATIENT, DOCTOR, and ADMIN roles with appropriate permissions. Doctors only access assigned patient data.
