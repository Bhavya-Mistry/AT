Our verification process

Becoming a ClinIQ physician is rigorous by design. We accept fewer than 20% of applicants.

1

Licence verification

Medical licence checked directly with state or national issuing authority.

2

Background check

Criminal background and malpractice history reviewed comprehensively.

3

Credential review

Board certifications and specialty credentials confirmed with issuing bodies.

4

Platform assessment

Standardised assessment on telehealth best practices and platform protocols.

5

Supervised onboarding

First 20 consultations reviewed by a senior medical advisor.

6

Ongoing monitoring

Continuous patient satisfaction tracking. Underperforming physicians are reviewed monthly.

Are you a licensed physician? Contact us to learn about joining the ClinIQ network. We're always looking for exceptional clinicians who share our commitment to accessible, high-quality care.
