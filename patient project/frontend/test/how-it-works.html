The three-step process

ClinIQ is designed around one principle: getting you the right care, fast. No waiting rooms, no phone trees, no uncertainty.

01
Instant · Free

Describe your symptoms

Open the AI consultation from your Patient Portal and describe how you're feeling in plain language. Our AI — trained on clinical guidelines and medical literature — asks follow-up questions to understand your situation fully. No medical jargon required.

02
Within minutes

Get triaged and matched

The AI generates a preliminary assessment and, where appropriate, connects you to a verified doctor in the relevant specialty. You can upload medical files — lab results, scan images, previous prescriptions — directly in the chat for the doctor to review.

03
Secure · Permanent

Receive care and keep your records

Your doctor reviews the AI's assessment, asks any additional questions, and issues a prescription or referral directly through the platform. Everything — the consultation, prescriptions, and uploaded files — is stored permanently in your encrypted health record.

How our AI works

The ClinIQ AI assistant is a clinical decision-support tool, not a replacement for medical expertise. It does three things exceptionally well:

  • Symptom collection — structured, empathetic intake that doesn't miss important details
  • Preliminary triage — risk stratification based on clinical protocols, flagging urgent cases immediately
  • Context packaging — preparing a concise case summary for the reviewing physician, saving them time and improving accuracy

Important: The AI never issues diagnoses or prescriptions. All clinical decisions are made by licensed physicians. The AI is your advocate — making sure the doctor has everything they need to help you effectively.

Doctor verification

Every physician on ClinIQ goes through a rigorous onboarding process before seeing a single patient:

  • Verification of medical licence with the issuing authority
  • Background and malpractice history check
  • Specialty credential review
  • Platform training and assessment
  • Ongoing peer review and patient satisfaction monitoring

Doctors are rated after every consultation. Those who consistently underperform are reviewed and, if necessary, removed from the platform. Quality of care is not negotiable.

Your health records

Everything in ClinIQ builds a single, longitudinal health record that belongs to you. At any time you can:

  • Download your complete medical history as a PDF
  • Share specific records with external providers via a secure link
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • See a full audit log of every access event on your records
