Getting started

How do I create an account? +
Click "Get started" on the landing page or "Create one" on the login screen. Enter your email and choose a password. You'll be logged in immediately. We recommend completing your health profile before your first consultation so the AI has context to work with.
Is ClinIQ free to use? +
Basic consultations and health record storage are free. Some specialist consultations or premium features may require a fee, which is always shown clearly before you proceed. There are no hidden charges.
What countries is ClinIQ available in? +
ClinIQ is currently available to residents of the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. We are expanding to additional regions. Sign up to be notified when we launch in a new country.
Can I use ClinIQ for my child? +
Patients aged 13–17 can use ClinIQ with parental consent provided during account creation. For children under 13, a parent or guardian must create and manage the account on their behalf. Paediatric specialists are available in our network.

Consultations

How does the AI consultation work? +
From your portal, click "AI Consultation" in the sidebar. Describe your symptoms in plain language — as you would to a nurse or GP. The AI will ask follow-up questions to build a clear picture, then triage your case and connect you with an appropriate physician. The AI does not diagnose or prescribe; it prepares your case for the doctor.
How long does it take to see a doctor? +
Most patients are connected with a physician within 15–30 minutes during peak hours. Urgent cases flagged by the AI triage system are prioritised. Outside peak hours, response times are typically under 10 minutes.
Can I upload files during a consultation? +
Yes. During any consultation you can upload JPEG, PNG, WebP, and PDF files up to 25 MB each. This includes lab results, scan images, photos of skin conditions, and previous prescriptions. Files are immediately visible to your assigned doctor.
What if my condition requires an in-person visit? +
Some conditions genuinely require physical examination or on-site testing. If your doctor determines an in-person visit is necessary, they will tell you clearly and may provide a referral. ClinIQ does not pressure doctors to treat conditions remotely when it is not appropriate.
Are prescriptions legally valid? +
Prescriptions issued by ClinIQ physicians are legally valid in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, subject to jurisdiction-specific rules. Controlled substances may not be prescribable via telehealth in some states. Your pharmacist can confirm validity for your location.

Health records

How do I access my consultation history? +
Your full consultation history is accessible from the "Sessions" section of your Patient Portal. Each session includes the full conversation, any uploaded files, and the prescription if one was issued. Sessions are stored permanently.
Can I download my records? +
Yes. From your health profile or session history, you can export your records as a structured PDF. You can also share specific records with external healthcare providers via a secure, time-limited link.
How do I delete my account and data? +
Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. This initiates a deletion request. Health records are retained for the legally required minimum period (7 years in most jurisdictions) before permanent deletion. Non-health data is deleted immediately. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Billing & payments

Is ClinIQ free to use? +
ClinIQ is currently free to use during our beta phase. All AI consultations, doctor appointments, and health record storage are available at no cost. We will notify users well in advance of any pricing changes.
Will there be paid features in the future? +
We may introduce premium features in the future, but basic healthcare access will remain free. Any paid features will be clearly marked, and you'll never be charged without explicit consent.
How will I know if billing is added? +
We will email all users at least 30 days before introducing any paid features. You'll have the option to continue using the free service or upgrade to premium features.

Account settings

How do I update my health profile? +
Your health profile includes your full name, contact number, address, blood group, and current medical status. You can update this information through the Patient Portal. Changes are saved immediately and available for your next consultation.
How do I change my password? +
Password changes are currently managed through the authentication system. We use bcrypt password hashing for security. If you need to reset your password, use the "Forgot password" option on the login page.
Can I add a profile picture? +
Yes. You can upload a profile picture (JPEG, PNG, or WebP format) through the Patient Portal. Pictures are stored securely and visible to doctors during consultations. You can update or remove your picture at any time.
How do I enable two-factor authentication? +
Two-factor authentication is not yet implemented in our system, but the infrastructure exists in our database. We recommend using a strong, unique password and keeping your account secure. We'll notify users when 2FA becomes available.
Can I use Google to sign in? +
Yes. We support Google OAuth for authentication. You can sign in with your Google account, which will automatically create a ClinIQ account if you're a new user. Google users don't need to set a separate password.

Privacy & security

Is my health data secure? +
Yes. All health data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Our platform is HIPAA-compliant and built on SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. See our Security page for a complete overview.
Do you sell my data? +
Never. We do not sell, rent, or trade your health information to any third party — not to insurers, advertisers, pharmaceutical companies, or data brokers. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
Who can see my health records? +
Only you and the physician assigned to your active consultation can see your health records. ClinIQ support staff cannot read your messages or records. All access is logged and visible to you under Account Settings → Audit Log.
